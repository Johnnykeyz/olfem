About Us

Fast-growing establishment delivering excellence in consulting and construction services

Olfem Integrated Services is a fast-growing establishment that started its consulting and contracting services in 2017. We have built a reputation for competent work teams, efficiency in service delivery, effective operations, quality jobs, and timely delivery.

Our team has carried out many challenging projects over the years, exposing us to complex work situations that have sharpened our abilities with necessary skills, know-how, and experience in design and build solutions, construction project management services, building trades, and related engineering works.

We are committed to repositioning ourselves in a more organized structure to serve our clients better, offering design inputs across various project stages and engineering solutions as value-added services.
